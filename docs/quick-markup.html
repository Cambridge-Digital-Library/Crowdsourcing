Quick Mark-up

A quick guide to our simple transcription mark-up

This is a quick guide to the basic “mark-up” indicators we would like you to use in your transcriptions.

You might want to copy and paste these into a simple text file on your computer and keep them close at hand to copy and paste directly into your transcriptions to save you repeatedly typing them out.

[BREAK]
= Page break

[BLANK]
= Blank page

[????]
= Illegible or unclear text

[!!!!]
= Deleted or obscured text

[SYMBOL]
= Symbols and non-standard characters

[DIAG]
= Presence and location of any graphical material (sketch, diagrams, photo etc.)

[TABLE]
= Use at beginning and end of any tabulated data
